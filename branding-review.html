03 — Photo Direction

Photography Mood

The photos on your site set the tone for your entire brand. Which mood matches the feeling you want clients to get?

Dark & Editorial

Deep backgrounds, dramatic lighting, studio-quality feel. Think magazine editorial. Makes your work look high-fashion and exclusive. Great with your current dark site theme.

Bright & Natural

Natural light, soft tones, warm and inviting. Think golden hour by the window. Makes your work feel approachable and personal. Great for showing the real you.

Earthy & Organic

Warm neutrals, linen textures, plant accents. Think holistic wellness studio. Matches your "hairstyling naturally" philosophy. Calm, grounded, and intentional.
