Meet Sophie

Hi, I'm Sophie — the hands and heart behind Miss Messy Bun. I'm a holistic loctician and natural stylist who believes your hair journey should feel as good as it looks.

Whether you're starting locs, maintaining protective styles, or looking for natural hair care that aligns with your values — I bring patience, plant-based products, and genuine care to every appointment.
