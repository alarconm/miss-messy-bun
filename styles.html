Next Steps & Suggestions

These came from a thorough design review of the live site. Some are things we can do right now, others need your input.

Needs Your Input

  • Portfolio photos need upgrading — Two of the four gallery images are Instagram screenshots with text overlays ("Salt & Pepper Extension Vibes"). For a professional portfolio, we should use clean, high-resolution photos with no IG formatting. Do you have the original photos from those posts?
  • Gallery feels sparse — Only 4 images in a 2x2 grid. Adding 2-4 more of your best work would make this section feel more substantial and impressive. Can you send your top 6-8 portfolio shots?
  • About section photo — The current photo has "The Artist" text and a painter emoji baked into the image. It looks a bit casual for the editorial feel of the site. Do you have a clean headshot or portrait we could use instead? We could add a styled text overlay in HTML that matches the site typography.
  • Client testimonials — A scrolling strip of client quotes would add social proof and build trust. Do you have any client reviews or feedback we could feature?

Quick Wins We Can Do Anytime

  • Holographic CTA button — The "Book Your Appointment" button at the bottom uses a gradient that can make white text hard to read in lighter spots. We added a text shadow to help, but we could also switch to a solid purple button for stronger readability.
  • Service categories — Are these 6 categories feeling right? We can easily rename, reorder, or split/merge them. Just say the word.
  • More photo breaks — We could add another full-width photo between the Gallery and Location sections to keep the visual rhythm going.

Future Ideas

  • Before & After gallery — A slider or side-by-side section showing transformation photos. Huge for conversion — clients love seeing the journey.
  • Seasonal menu highlights — A rotating banner or featured service section for seasonal specials (e.g., "Spring Refresh: Herbal Steam + Retwist Bundle").
  • Instagram feed integration — Auto-pull your latest IG posts into the site so the portfolio stays fresh without manual updates.
  • FAQ section — Common questions about locs, booking, pricing, first visits. Reduces DM back-and-forth and builds confidence.
